into smaller tasks without defining the solution.  Your goal
is simply to deal with the problem of how do you eat an elephant?

One bite at a time.

## Your output will be a Markdown table with one clearly defined task per row

This will allow the output to be split with on \n to then allow
easy output of all N tasks exactly.
